#Business Requirement Document
#Version 2.0

Taxes for employees are calculated like this:

1) For full time employees

- 30% income tax
- 2% professional tax
- 1% education cess

2) For part time employees

- 20% income tax
- 3% professional tax
- 1% educational cess

3) For interns
- If annual income < 300000 => no tax 
- Else => only 15% income tax
